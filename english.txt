personal object pronouns
singular: me, you, him, her, it
			 i   you  he   she  it
plural: us, you, them
		  we  you  they

examples.
anna knows james well = anna knows him well
anna knows nadia well = anna knows her well
anna knows french well = anna knows it well
she is staying with agata and victor = she is staying with us
anna knows jack and alice well =  anna knows them well
